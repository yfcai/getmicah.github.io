Software Projects

[Tutorial Assignment] This Scala console application scheduled tutorials for "Informatik 1", a 400-student lecture at the university of Tübingen. It integrates with the lecture's forum based on Discourse. Students and tutors enter their time tables as a forum setting; the teaching staff enter the availability of rooms. The software assigns students and tutors to rooms and timeslots such that all tutors have similar numbers of students. The scheduling problem is then encoded as a minimum-cost-flow instance with edge activation constraints, to be solved by Gurobi via Optimus.
